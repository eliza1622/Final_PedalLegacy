Our History

The history of the Pedal Legacy system is deeply rooted in the long evolution of cycling, which began with the invention of the first steerable two-wheeler in 1817. Just as the original "Laufmaschine" was created to provide a new way to navigate the world, this digital platform was established to help the Cebuano cycling community navigate the modern race landscape.

While the bicycle evolved through the 19th century from the "Boneshaker" of 1863 to the first "Safety Bicycle" in 1885. Pedal Legacy represents the next step in that journey by integrating technology with tradition.

The system serves as a centralized hub for race management, bridging the gap between event organizers and athletes who previously lacked a unified registration tool. By simplifying complex logistics and offering a seamless digital workflow, the platform honors the spirit of innovation that has defined cycling for over two centuries.

A key milestone in its development was the implementation of a secure user authentication system, allowing participants to track their personal registration history alongside modern features like interactive trail maps. The mission behind the system is driven by local enthusiasts who understand the unique challenges of the Cebu cycling scene, from mountain trails to coastal roads.

Ultimately, Pedal Legacy stands as a digital testament to an enduring passion that started with a wooden frame in 1817 and continues to thrive in the heart of the Philippines today.
